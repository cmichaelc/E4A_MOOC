## Grade Entry

### Understanding the Benin Grading Formula

**Formula:** `((Average Controls + Sum of Exams) / (Number of Exams + 1)) √ó Coefficient`

**Components:**
- **Controls:** Regular assessments, quizzes, classwork
- **Exams:** Major tests, mid-term, final exams
- **Coefficient:** Subject weight (set by manager)

**Example:**
- Controls: 12, 14, 13 ‚Üí Average = 13
- Exams: 15, 16 ‚Üí Sum = 31, Count = 2
- Coefficient: 2
- **Final Grade:** `((13 + 31) / (2 + 1)) √ó 2 = 29.33`

### Enter Grades for a Class

**Step 1:** Click **"Grade Entry"** or your class card

**Step 2:** Select the subject (if you teach multiple)

**Step 3:** Click **"Enter Grades"** or **"Add Grade"**

**Step 4:** Fill in the form:
- **Student:** Select from dropdown
- **Type:** Choose "Control" or "Exam"
- **Score:** Enter grade (0-20 scale)
- **Date:** When the assessment was given

**Step 5:** Click **"Submit"**

## Parent Communication

### Send a Message to a Parent

**Step 1:** Click **"Messages"**

**Step 2:** Click **"New Message"**

**Step 3:** Fill in:
- **Select Student:** Choose student (parent receives message)
- **Subject:** Clear topic line
- **Message:** Your message content

**Step 4:** Click **"Send"**

### Reply to Messages

1. Go to **Messages**
2. Click on conversation
3. Type your reply
4. Click **"Send"**

### Message Best Practices

**Do:**
- Be professional and respectful
- Respond within 24-48 hours
- Keep parents informed of issues
- Celebrate student achievements

**Don't:**
- Share other students' information
- Use informal language
- Ignore urgent messages
- Discuss sensitive matters only via messaging

## Understanding Academic Terms

### What are Terms?

Terms (semesters/trimesters) divide the academic year into periods.

**Your school may use:**
- 2 Semesters
- 3 Trimesters
- 4 Quarters

### Current Term

**Important:** Grades you enter are automatically assigned to the **current term**.

**Check Current Term:**
- Look for announcement from manager

### Per-Term Reports

Students can download:

## Best Practices

### Grading

**Frequency:**
- Enter grades **immediately** after assessment
- Don't wait until end of term
- Regular entry = accurate reports

**Accuracy:**
- Double-check scores before submitting
- Use correct grade type (Control vs Exam)
- Verify student selection

**Balance:**
- Mix of controls and exams
- At least 3-4 controls per term
- 1-2 exams per term for accurate formula

### Attendance

**Daily Routine:**
- Mark attendance at start of class
- Update if student arrives late
- Mark excused absences appropriately

**Follow-up:**
- Contact parents after 3 consecutive absences
- Document attendance issues
- Report patterns to administration

### Communication

**Proactive:**
- Share weekly/monthly updates
- Notify about upcoming exams
- Celebrate good performance

**Responsive:**
- Reply to parent messages promptly
- Address concerns professionally
- Escalate serious issues to manager

## Common Questions

**Q: Can I enter grades for past dates?**  
A: Yes, select the assessment date when entering the grade

**Q: Student changed classes, what about their grades?**  
A: Grades stay with the student across class changes

**Q: Made a mistake in grade entry, can I fix it?**  
A: Yes, find the grade and click "Edit" to update

**Q: Parent not receiving my messages?**  
A: Verify student is linked to parent account (check with manager)

**Q: Don't see all my students in grade entry?**  
A: Verify you're in the correct class and subject
