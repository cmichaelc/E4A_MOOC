**Primary Responsibilities:** School approvals, system oversight, platform management

---

## Dashboard Overview

The admin dashboard shows:
- **Pending Schools** - Schools awaiting approval (highlighted in yellow)
- **Approved Schools** - Active schools on the platform
- **Rejected Schools** - Schools that were denied
- **Quick Actions** - Manage Schools, View Reports

---

## School Approval Workflow

### Viewing Pending Schools

1. From dashboard, click **"Manage Schools"** or **"Pending Schools"** card
2. You'll see a list of all schools with their status
3. **Yellow badges** = Pending review
4. **Green badges** = Approved
5. **Red badges** = Rejected

### Approving a School

**Step 1:** Click **"View Details"** or **"Approve"** on a pending school

**Step 2:** Review the information:
- School name and address
- Manager contact details
- Phone and email
- Registration date

**Step 3:** Click **"Approve"** button

**Step 4:** Confirm the approval

**Result:** 
- School status changes to "Approved"
- Manager receives notification
- Manager can now access full features

### Rejecting a School

**Step 1:** Click **"Reject"** on a pending school

**Step 2:** **IMPORTANT:** Provide a clear rejection reason
- Be specific about why (e.g., "Incomplete address information")
- Be professional and helpful
- Suggest what needs to be corrected

**Step 3:** Submit rejection

**Result:**
- School status changes to "Rejected"
- Manager receives notification with your reason
- Manager can edit information and resubmit

### Resubmissions

When a manager resubmits after rejection:
1. School appears in **Pending** list again
2. Review updated information
3. Approve if corrections are satisfactory
4. Reject again if still insufficient (provide new feedback)

## Troubleshooting

### "No pending schools showing"
✅ This is normal - means all schools are processed

### "Manager can't access features after approval"
1. Verify school status is "Approved" (green)
2. Ask manager to log out and log back in
3. Check manager's email is verified

### "School resubmitted multiple times"
1. Review your rejection reasons
2. Ensure feedback is specific enough
3. Consider direct communication with manager
