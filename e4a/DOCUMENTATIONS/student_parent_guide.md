### Common Questions

**Q: Can I see real-time grades?**  
A: Yes! Grades appear as soon as teachers enter them

**Q: Why don't I see some subjects?**  
A: Teachers may not have entered grades yet for that subject

**Q: Attendance looks wrong**  
A: Contact school office - they can verify and correct

**Q: How do I know there's a new message?**  
A: Check the Messages icon - you'll see unread count

**Q: Can I message multiple teachers?**  
A: Yes, send separate messages to each teacher

**Q: Report card won't download**  
A: Ensure PDF reader is installed; try different browser
